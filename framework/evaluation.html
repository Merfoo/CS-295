Design

Page layout and spacing

The layout of the website is very well thought out, the only recommendation I would make is to perhaps make the posts column a little more narrow.

Functionality of navigation, forms, and widgets

The navigation was nice and easy to use. It was not confusing to navigate around the site and each link was clear and concise in where it would take you.

Color scheme

I like the shade of blue and balance of colors between them on the website.

Logo (symbol and wordmark) design

The logo looks cool but its a little difficult to know what it is.

Browser compatibility

The website looked fine in the latest versions of Edge, Google Chrome, and Firefox.

Content

Descriptive title tag

It appears the currently the title tag is just the website name on posts and user pages.

Descriptive navigation items

The navigation items were very descriptive of where they would take you.

Descriptive headlines and subheadlines

The headlines and subheadlines were concise and the hierarchy made sense.

Grammatically correct writing

Most of the content is submitted by users, but it looks like the content is grammatically correct.

Use of keyword phrases that promote the site

The only keyword phrases I could find was in the navbar, but I think thats the slogan.

Policy page completeness

The policy is really well written and thorough!

Helpful language for all interactive functions (forms)

All the forms were easy and pretty straightforward to use.

Value of content related to the ecommerce page.

The UI of the subscription page is really nice, I especially like the two column layout. The value from subscribing is pretty high since the money goes towards keeping the server up.
